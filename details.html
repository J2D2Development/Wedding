Lodging   Where to stay

Portland is an amazing city with a whole lot of awesome things to do. The venue is about 15 minutes Northwest of town, so we've provided some recommended hotels in NW Portland.
  • The Inn at Northrup Station is in the heart of NW Portland. Quirky. Pet Friendly. Portland.
  • The Silver Cloud Inn is closest to the wedding venue, but still walkable to trendy 23rd street. And close to Forest Park if you want to run or explore.
  • If, like the groom, you prefer the anonymity of a major chain (as well as the free continental breakfast), the NW Holiday Inn Express might be right up your alley!
Be sure to check services like airbnb or vrbo for other options!

About Town   Explore Portland

If you have time while you're in town, be sure to check out something cool
  • Powell's City of Books- an entire city block of books!
  • Coming in on Thursday the 9th? Plan ahead and get tickets to a Timbers home game (buy early or take your chances)!
  • Forest Park is the biggest urban greenspace in the US- it's cool. Go for a jog or hike and be astounded by trees and dirt!
  • If you're in town for a few days, a short drive to Mt. Hood's Timberline Lodge won't disappoint. All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy!
